Network Administrator

An IT professional who can ensure the company's network is stable and secure while also providing technical support.

Reference Number: NA942

Salary range: $3000-$5000

Reports to: Senior IT Manager

Key Responsibilities

  • Monitor and maintain network performance
  • Troubleshoot connectivity issues
  • Manage firewalls, routers, and VPN systems
  • Ensure network is secure

Qualifications

Essential

  1. Bachelor's degree in IT
  2. Minimum 3 years of work experience in the industry
  3. Certification in CompTIA
  4. Basic knowledge of cloud networking and network protocols

Preferred Skills

  • Problem-solving
  • Time management
  • Critical thinking
